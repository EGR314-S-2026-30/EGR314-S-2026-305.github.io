## Team Video Introduction

This video presents our team’s exploration rover and the goals behind its design and development. The project focuses on creating a small robotic platform capable of exploring an environment while collecting useful data. Our team worked together to design, build, and test the rover to demonstrate its movement and overall functionality. In this video, we showcase the rover in action and highlight the results of our project.
